About Us

We are a non-kill shelter who’s main focus is on pet care, education and love. We envision a world where every pet is loved, cared for and understood. Our pet’s are living beings, they are a part of our families. So we thrive to show people these facts and keep our community properly informed.

Our shelter is big on community outreach and volunteering. The opportunity to get more people involved helps us maintain the shelter while giving the chance to people who love pets but are not in a situation to adopt to still make a difference in their lives!
