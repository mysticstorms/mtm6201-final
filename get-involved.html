Positions

Here are some volunteer positions you may be interested in joining. We take casual, part-time and full-time volunteers. We do not discriminate, anybody who wants to help can! We always hope to grow our team and create a lovely family with strong values. You always have the opportunity to make a difference!

Animal Caretaker Assistant

2+ hrs a week | 1 day a week

Help maintain and clean the shelter independently and responsibly. The Animal Care Assistant will be responsible for daily housekeeping tasks to maintain a clean, pleasant and healthy environment for the animals.

LEARN MORE

Counsellor's Assistant

2+ hrs a week | 1 day a week

Provide essential support by performing various tasks, including: Assisting with managing animal records, completing necessary forms, updating animal information, and ensuring documentation management.

Cat Welfare Volunteer

2+ hrs a week | 1 day a week

Ensure the well-being of the cats with passion and care, while respecting the rules of the shelter. The job involves contributing to their comfort and safety, as well as meeting their daily needs, whether physical or emotional.
